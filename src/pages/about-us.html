# Our Story

About Hamdard Academy

Founded in 2001 by respected educator Shahid Ameer Shahid, Hamdard Academy has been a beacon of academic support for school-going students in SadiqAbad.

For over two decades, our mission has remained the same: to provide affordable, high-quality education that strengthens school learning and prepares students for success in board exams.

Under the leadership of our honorable Principal, Hamdard Academy continues to deliver concept clarity, discipline, and progress-focused learning in a trusted environment.

Our Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every class, every test, and every student we teach.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality education that strengthens school learning, builds strong concepts, and prepares every student — Science or Arts — for success in board examinations.

Our Vision

To be SadiqAbad's most trusted academy, known for disciplined learning, consistent testing, and results that open doors to a brighter future for every family we serve.

Our Core Values

What sets the Hamdard Academy classroom experience apart.

Concept Clarity

We build understanding from the ground up so students grasp the "why," not just the "what."

Discipline & Consistency

Regular classes and a fixed testing rhythm keep every student on track throughout the year.

Science & Arts, Equally

Our programs give both streams the same structured attention — no group is treated as secondary.

Structured Testing System

Our 100-Test Plan keeps students exam-ready with regular, syllabus-wide assessments.

Affordable Fees

Quality education shouldn't be out of reach — we keep fees reasonable with flexible options.

Personal Attention

Separate, focused classes for boys and girls mean every student is seen and supported.
